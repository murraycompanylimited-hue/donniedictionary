Word of the Day

Authority

Standard

The right or power to give orders, make decisions, or enforce obedience.

Legal / Commercial

A recognized source of lawful command arising from competent jurisdiction and consent.

Cultural / Street

Who actually runs it when the dust settles.

Use it to win:

Ask: “By what authority are you acting? Show the lawful basis for your demand.” If they can’t answer, authority collapses.
